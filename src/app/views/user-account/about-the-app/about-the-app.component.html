ABOUT THE APP

LEARNigeria Remedial Programme (LRP)

This web-based LEARNigeria Remedial Programme (LRP) app is developed by TEP Centre using interactive literacy and numeracy content created from the Nigerian curriculum. The digital remedial app will be able to track a childâ€™s self-progress, providing remedial intervention remotely and accessible to every child. This is in a bit to expand in the implementation of LRP in circumstances where it is challenging to have in-person instruction with learners and there are restrictions to social interaction and schooling. The content for LRP app is curated from the LRP/KOYA curriculum as well as the activities that have been recorded and documented for deployment before the pandemic.

This innovation will enable TEP Centre to provide digital remedial learning to children with minimal need for instructors. Similarly, as an extension for continuous resourcing and capacity building, TEP Centre seeks to automate its learning academy platform (TEPLA) to provide digital capacity development in the areas of research, assessment, learning interventions, and advocacy in the education sector. TEPLA would be able to provide resources that can be accessed across Nigeria and beyond.

In Nigeria, remedial learning interventions have been conducted primarily as in-person instructor-led programs in schools as well as out-of-school holiday camps in local communities. COVID 19 pandemic led TEP to re-strategise how best to redesign our remedial learning interventions to adapt to the changes in how children learn in a pandemic and post-pandemic world.
